honyaku.dev is an Internationalization (i18n) tool that helps you generate localization files using AI.
You can either manually upload, edit, export localization files on the dashboard, or integrate our API into your development workflow to automatically generate localization files as you develop.

For pricing information, visit the [pricing page](/pricing).

In most cases, you want to integrate honyaku.dev into your development workflow to automatically generate localization files as you develop.
